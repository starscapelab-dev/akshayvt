  Common questions

Questions you're probably about to ask.

If yours isn't here, send it on WhatsApp. I read every message myself — not a chatbot, not a VA.

Ask a question →
How long does it take to build a website?
A standard business website takes 5–10 days. E-commerce or custom web apps may take 2–4 weeks depending on scope. I always commit to a timeline upfront, in writing.
Do you work with clients in UAE and Dubai?
Yes. Most of my current work is for clients in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Sharjah. Remote, WhatsApp-first, and timezone-friendly (UAE and India sit within an hour of each other).
Can you fix or update my existing website?
Absolutely — fixes, speed improvements and ongoing maintenance are some of my most in-demand services. Share the URL and I'll give you an honest assessment, free.
Will my website rank on Google?
Every site I build includes technical SEO, schema markup, semantic HTML, fast loading and Core Web Vitals tuning — the foundations Google actually rewards. Rankings then depend on content and competition, but the technical groundwork will be solid.
Do you offer maintenance after launch?
Yes. Flexible monthly retainers — no long lock-in contracts. Includes updates, security patches, uptime monitoring and small content changes.
What technologies do you use?
React and Next.js for most modern builds, with TypeScript and Tailwind. WordPress, PHP, Shopify and custom APIs when they suit the project. I pick the right tool — not the trendiest one.
How do I get started?
Send a WhatsApp message, fill the contact form, or email info@akshayvt.com. Free consultation, no commitment. I usually reply within a few hours.
